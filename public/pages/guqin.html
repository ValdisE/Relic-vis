士人无故不撤琴瑟
The literati did not withdraw the guqin for no reason

Guqin has a long and profound voice, and is simple and introverted. The literati in the past dynasty not only “does n’t withdraw without any reason”, but also pays great attention to the educational role of Guqin. Playing the piano is not only a way of self-cultivation, but also a sustenance of their heart and voice. Wang Fuzhi, Zuo Zongtang, and Tan Zong are equally harmonious with Huqin sages and Guqin. They are lifelong companions, adding a strong touch to the Guqin culture in Huxiang area.

琴器
The structure of Guqin

The standard length of Guqin is three feet, six inches and five minutes, corresponding to 365 degrees on Sundays and 365 days in a year. The thirteen emblems on the body are imitated by twelve months a year and a leap month, and the flat shape below the circle above it represents the universe view of the ancestors' round circle. Although Guqin is small, it has all the internal organs and contains Heaven, earth, man and time are very much in harmony with the aesthetic view of the unity of heaven and man.

The shape of Guqin

There is a saying in the field of Guqin: "The shape of the Guqin in the Tang Dynasty is more round, and the shape of the Guqin in the Song Dynasty is flatter." Guqin has a beautiful shape.There’re more than 50 styles of Guqin records over the years. The difference between the same styles is slight, but the neck and waist of the Guqin are concavely curved, reflecting the constantly developing cultural concepts of each era.

减字谱

中国古琴常用的一种以记写指位与左右手演奏技法为特征的记谱法。 这种记谱法使用减字拼成某种符号记录左手按弦指法和右手弹奏指法，它是一种只记录演奏法和音高，不记录音名、节奏的记谱法。减字谱是对文字谱记谱法的一次重大改革，是一种沿用千年而未被取代的古老记谱法。

右手“八法”

右手“八法”为古琴最基本的八种指法，这八种指法或紧、或慢、或多、或少等诸多不同的组合，共同构成了右手其它的指法。其他指法实由八法变化组合而成。

适宜弹琴的场合

根据《正文对音捷要真传琴谱》记载，有十四种场合是适宜弹琴。逢知己，遇可人，对道士，处高堂，升楼阁，在宫观，坐石上，登山埠，憩空谷，游水湄，居舟中，息林下，二气清朗，风清月明等。

九大琴派

琴文化本身就是一种非常个性化的艺术，不同地域、不同师承的琴家的风格彼此都不相同。同一地域、同一师承的琴家的风格也是各有特点而不尽相同。同一曲目，不同的琴家就会有不同的理解，而形成不同的风格。所以，在琴的领域，也同样有流派之说。
